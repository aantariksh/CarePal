About CarePal Money

CarePal Money is a Lending Service Provider (LSP) that aims to bring holistic solutions in healthcare financing through patient-centric lending schemes. At CarePal Money, in partnership with RBI regulated entities/ NBFCs, get financial help to cover your healthcare needs with flexible payment options that are just the right fit for you.

All CarePal and related lending schemes offer easy and pocket-friendly EMIs, helping you receive the right care at the right time without any additional financial strain. Focusing on transparency and customer satisfaction, we aim to make elective medical treatments accessible and affordable for one and all.
